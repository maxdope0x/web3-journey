CryptoZombies Journey — Visualized!
I’m excited to share that I’ve successfully visualized the entire end‑to‑end flow of my CryptoZombies project.

Instead of diving straight into Solidity, I built a mind map and comic storyboard that turned abstract concepts into playful visuals:  
- Smart Contract = Factory with unchangeable rules.  
- Array = Storage Room where zombies live in numbered lockers.  
- Functions = Machines that create, train, and battle.  
- Variables = Sticky Notes showing name, DNA, and level.  
- Events = Loudspeaker announcing when zombies level up.  
- Blockchain = Transparent City where everyone can see inside.  

This visualization helped me understand the logic in a child‑friendly way:  
- User gives a zombie name.  
- The machine generates random DNA.  
- A new zombie is created, stored, and announced to the city.  

By sketching the factory, machines, and zombies, I now have a clear mental picture of what my code will achieve.  

---

 Takeaway: Before coding, visualize. It makes the abstract concrete, and turns learning into play.
